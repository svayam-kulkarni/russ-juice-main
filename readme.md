RUSS Cold Pressed Juice is a premium, small-batch beverage brand focused on delivering fresh, nutrient-rich juices with an emphasis on quality and process. Its core product, a cold-pressed watermelon, beetroot, and carrot (WBC) blend, is designed to provide natural hydration and sustained energy without added sugar or preservatives. By using cold-pressing instead of conventional blending, the juice retains more nutrients, flavour, and freshness, creating a clear point of differentiation.

The business operates on a pre-order model with limited drops, ensuring strict quality control while creating a sense of exclusivity. Each batch is positioned as crafted rather than mass-produced, supporting a premium yet accessible pricing strategy based on perceived value.

Marketing is driven through WhatsApp channels and word-of-mouth, allowing for direct, targeted reach and stronger trust. This approach builds a loyal, health-conscious customer base seeking a clean and energising start to their day, reinforcing RUSS as a curated wellness brand rather than a typical beverage product.
