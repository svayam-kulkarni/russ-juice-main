Our Story

Why RUSS exists

Most juices on the market are a compromise. They're centrifugally blended (which generates heat and degrades nutrients), packed with added sugar to compensate for lost flavour, or sitting on a shelf for days with preservatives keeping them "fresh".

RUSS was built to fix that. Cold pressing extracts juice through pressure alone, no heat, no oxidation, no nutrient loss. The result is a bottle that tastes more alive because it actually is. And because we only produce what's ordered, there's no wastage, no guessing, and no cutting corners on quality.

Every batch is small by design. That's not a limitation, it's the whole point.
